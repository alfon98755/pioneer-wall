# Privacy Policy

**Pi Pioneer Wall**  
**Last updated:** June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pi Pioneer Wall ("we", "us", or "the Platform") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our web application and related services.

By using Pi Pioneer Wall, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information you provide
When you use the Platform, you may provide:

- **Display name** (pioneer username shown on the wall and leaderboard)
- **Message content** (text displayed when you claim the throne)
- **Bid amounts** (Test-Pi values you enter when placing bids)

### 1.2 Automatically collected information
We may automatically collect:

- Browser type and version
- Device type and operating system
- IP address (for security, abuse prevention, and analytics)
- Session data and usage timestamps
- Error logs and performance data

### 1.3 Blockchain and wallet data (when connected)
If the Platform is connected to Pi Network or a wallet in the future, we may process:

- Wallet address or Pi username
- Transaction hashes and on-chain payment records
- Test-Pi or Pi payment amounts related to bids and rewards

We do **not** collect or store private keys, seed phrases, or wallet passwords.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Operate the throne-bidding and leaderboard features
- Process bids, pool updates, king rewards, and platform fees
- Display your name, message, and ranking on the Platform
- Prevent fraud, abuse, and manipulation of the bidding system
- Improve performance, security, and user experience
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 3. How We Share Information

We do **not** sell your personal information.

We may share information:

- **Publicly on the Platform** — your pioneer name, message, bid totals, and leaderboard position are visible to other users
- **With service providers** — hosting, analytics, or payment infrastructure partners under confidentiality obligations
- **For legal reasons** — when required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect rights and safety
- **In a business transfer** — if the Platform is merged, acquired, or reorganized

On-chain transactions, once implemented, may be publicly visible on the Pi Network blockchain by design.

---

## 4. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to:

- Provide the Platform and maintain leaderboard history
- Resolve disputes and enforce our Terms of Service
- Meet legal and accounting requirements

You may request deletion of off-chain account data where applicable, subject to legal limits and the immutability of public blockchain records.

---

## 5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and access controls.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You use the Platform at your own risk.

---

## 6. Cookies and Local Storage

The Platform may use:

- **Cookies** — for session management and preferences
- **Local storage** — to save UI state or demo data in the MVP version

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work correctly.
