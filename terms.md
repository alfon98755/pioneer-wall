## 4. Bidding Rules

### 4.1 Minimum bid
- If no King holds the throne, the minimum opening bid is **10 Test-Pi** (unless otherwise stated on the Platform).
- To dethrone the current King, your bid must be at least **the current King's bid + 1 Test-Pi**.

### 4.2 Free bidding
You may bid any amount equal to or above the minimum. Higher bids increase your chance of claiming the throne immediately upon successful payment.

### 4.3 Failed bids
Bids below the minimum may not claim the throne. A portion of failed or insufficient bids may be allocated to the current King as throne earnings, as described on the Platform.

### 4.4 Finality
Successful bids are **final and non-refundable**, except where required by law or explicitly stated otherwise.
